Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 14, 2026

Summary: Carrousel Factory collects only the minimum data necessary to connect your social media accounts and publish content on your behalf. We do not sell your data. You can delete your data at any time.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Social Media Account Data

When you connect a social media account (TikTok, Instagram) via OAuth, we receive and store:

  • Profile information: username, display name, profile picture URL.
  • Authentication tokens: OAuth access tokens and refresh tokens, stored securely in our database.
  • Basic account metrics: follower count (if provided by the platform).

1.2 Content Data

We store the carousel content you create within the application, including images, captions, hashtags, and publication settings. This content is stored locally on your server instance.

1.3 Usage Data

We may collect basic usage information such as publication history and error logs to improve the Service. No analytics or tracking tools from third parties are used.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively to:

  • Authenticate your social media accounts via OAuth.
  • Publish carousel content to your connected social media accounts on your behalf.
  • Display your account status and token validity in the application.
  • Refresh expired authentication tokens to maintain connectivity.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties. Your data is only transmitted to:

  • TikTok API: to publish content and retrieve account information, as authorized by you.
  • Instagram/Meta Graph API: to publish content and retrieve account information, as authorized by you.

These transmissions occur only when you explicitly initiate a publication or connect an account.

4. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored in a local SQLite database on your server. Authentication tokens are stored in the database and are never exposed to the frontend or transmitted to any third party other than the respective social media platforms for authentication purposes.

5. Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account is connected. When you disconnect a social media account:

  • Authentication tokens are revoked with the platform (when supported).
  • The account is deactivated in our database.
  • Publication history is retained for your reference but can be deleted upon request.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all data associated with your connected accounts through the application interface.
  • Disconnect: Revoke access to any connected social media account at any time.
  • Delete: Request complete deletion of your data by disconnecting all accounts and removing the application.
  • Portability: Export your content data at any time.

7. Third-Party Platform Policies

Your use of connected social media platforms is also governed by their privacy policies:

  • TikTok Privacy Policy
  • Instagram Privacy Policy

8. Children's Privacy

Carrousel Factory is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Your continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us at contact@carrouselfactory.com.
